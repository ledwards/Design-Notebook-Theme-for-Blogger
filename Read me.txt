UPLOADING THE THEME
------------------------------------------------------------

1. Download your Blogger XML Theme 
   The theme is contained in a zip file (winzip, winrar), ensure you have extracted the XML theme.

2. Log in to your Blogger dashboard and go to Layout > Edit HTML

3. Ensure you back up your old theme in case you decide to use it again. To do this, click on the "download full theme" link and save the file to your hard drive.

4. Look for the section near the top where you can browse for your XML theme:

5. Enter the location of your theme and press "upload".

6. The HTML of your new theme will now appear in the box below. You can preview your theme or simply save to start using it!

7. Enjoy!
